Simple Interest

Calculate the interest:

\( I = P \times r \times t \)

Calculate the final amount:

\( A = P + I \)

  • \( I \) = interest (dollar amount)
  • \( P \) = principal (starting amount)
  • \( r \) = interest rate (decimal form)
  • \( t \) = time (years)
  • \( A \) = final amount (dollars)

Compound Interest

Calculate the final amount:

\( \displaystyle A = P \times \left(1 + \frac{r}{n}\right)^{n \times t} \)

Calculate the initial amount:

\( \displaystyle P = \frac{A}{\left(1 + \frac{r}{n}\right)^{n \times t}} \)

  • \( A \) = final amount (dollars)
  • \( P \) = principal (starting amount)
  • \( r \) = interest rate (decimal form)
  • \( n \) = number of compounding periods per year
  • \( t \) = time (years)

Savings Plan

Calculate the final amount saved:

\( \displaystyle A = PMT \times \frac{\left[\left(1 + \frac{r}{12}\right)^{12 \times t} - 1\right]}{\left(\frac{r}{12}\right)} \)

Calculate the monthly payment:

\( \displaystyle PMT = A \times \frac{\left(\frac{r}{12}\right)}{\left[\left(1 + \frac{r}{12}\right)^{12 \times t} - 1\right]} \)

  • \( A \) = final amount
  • \( PMT \) = monthly payment
  • \( r \) = interest rate (decimal form)
  • \( t \) = time (years)

Returns

Total Return:

\( \displaystyle R_{total} = \left(\frac{A - P}{P}\right) \times 100\% \)

Annual Return:

\( \displaystyle R_{annual} = \left[\left(\frac{A}{P}\right)^{1/t} - 1\right] \times 100\% \)

  • \( R \) = return
  • \( A \) = final amount
  • \( P \) = principal
  • \( t \) = time (years)

Loans

Monthly loan payment:

\( \displaystyle PMT = P \times \frac{\left(\frac{r}{12}\right)}{\left[1 - \left(1 + \frac{r}{12}\right)^{-(12 \times t)}\right]} \)

Total paid:

\( \displaystyle A = PMT \times 12 \times t \)

Interest paid:

\( \displaystyle I = A - P \)

  • \( PMT \) = monthly payment
  • \( A \) = total amount paid
  • \( P \) = principal (loan amount)
  • \( r \) = interest rate (decimal form)
  • \( t \) = loan term (years)

Credit Cards

Interest (finance charge):

\( \displaystyle I = r \times \frac{N}{365} \times AVG \)

New balance:

\( \displaystyle B_{new} = B_{previous} - PMT + C + I \)

  • \( B \) = balance (dollars)
  • \( I \) = interest (dollars)
  • \( r \) = interest rate (decimal form)
  • \( N \) = number of days in billing cycle
  • \( PMT \) = payment
  • \( C \) = new charges
  • \( AVG \) = average daily balance
